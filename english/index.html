Noun

The word or words used in the sentence that refer to the name of the person. animal, place or thing are known as noun.

Example: Ram, Sita.

Pro-noun

The word that is used in place of noun is called pronoun.

Example: he, she, it, they, you.

Verbs

Verb is that word or group of words in the sentence that specifies the action taken or the state of the subject.

Example: Play, read, write, count, sing, jump, run….
